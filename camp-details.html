Important Details:


  • The ITC is only guaranteed "2-PERSON, RESIDENTIAL SUITES" with extra-long, twin mattresses.

    • *Please list only ONE roommate during registration

  • We also provide an Extended Day Camp experience
  • $100 deposit or full payment must be mailed with application ($20 returned check fee).
    Make checks payable to: 'Intense Tennis Camps'.
  • Applications AFTER 5/21/21 MUST include full payment.
  • Applications AFTER 5/21/21 and International Customers MUST use PayPal or Venmo.
  • The balance due is receivable 21 days before the start of camp.
  • If you cancel a session more than 21 days from the camp start, there will be a full refund less a $50 service charge. Within 21 days, the deposit is non-refundable.

    • After camp starts - NO REFUNDS!

  • Camp is limited to 60 campers/week.
  • Campers are evaluated for compatibility on Sunday evening.
  • Campers attending 2+ weeks, same family members, SRU employees, and US Military Personnel will receive a $20 discount/week.
  • Camp rules and regulations are included in camp package and our ITC Playbook will be issued on Sunday evening.

On-Court Highlights

  • 30+ hours of instruction and play
  • Approximately 5:1 student-to-certified instructor ratio
  • Pro exhibitions
  • Fundamental skills - individually addressed
  • Daily active stretching and conditioning
  • Daily match play
  • Style and strategy development
  • Double tactics
  • Mental Toughness Training ('Intentional Listening Required')
  • USTA patterns of play
  • Team Training
  • Sports drinks, water, ice and water bottles provided
  • HEAD/Penn Demo Day

Off-Court Highlights

  • Daily sports science educational presentations
  • ITC 'Playbook' provided
  • Cross-training activities
  • Supervised evening activities
  • 'Around-the-clock' counselor supervision
  • Camp Package included: t-shirt, water bottles, certificate, awards, etc
