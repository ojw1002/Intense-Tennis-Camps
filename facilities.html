ABOUT SRU


Slippery Rock University was founded in 1889. It is well known for its beautiful, historic campus spreading over 660 rolling acres in Butler County. Intense Tennis Camps has been running Camps at this wonderful State College since 2004. Its community consists of approximately 3,500 people. As of 2019, Slippery Rock University was recognized as one of the safest colleges in the United States by the National Council for Home Safety and Security.


SRU was named one of the Top 25 Safest College Campuses in America in 2020. Click here to learn more.

COURTS & MORE


Slippery Rock University offers 12 outdoor tennis courts. 6 of those courts are lit and resurfaced. We also will travel to Grove City College. Grove City offers 8 new courts that have just been redone in 2018.
ITC is an outdoor camp. Indoor backup is not guaranteed, but the ITC will try their best!
