What are the Intense Tennis Camps?

Come join us for an 'All In' tennis camp experience. Everyday activities include: Play with Pro Exhibitions, USTA Sport Medicine Presentations, mental toughness training, explosive speed training and match play! The Intense Tennis Camps also hosts supervised activities that are monitored by certified and qualified team members.
We are located at Slippery Rock University, only about 50 miles north of Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania!
